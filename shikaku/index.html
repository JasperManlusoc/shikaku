How to Play
1
The Goal
Divide the entire grid into rectangles. Every cell must belong to exactly one rectangle — no gaps, no overlaps.
2
Numbered Cells
Each number = the area of the rectangle it belongs to. A "6" means a 2×3 or 1×6 rectangle covering exactly 6 cells.
3
Draw Rectangles
Desktop: Click and drag across cells.
Mobile: Touch and drag.
Erase: Tap any colored cell to remove its rectangle.
4
Auto-Check
No button needed! The puzzle detects a perfect solution instantly and celebrates
↩
Undo (3 uses)
Tap the pink ↩ button to undo your last rectangle. Up to 3 undos available.
